Taco Trivia

When did tacos first appear in India?

Chenna Reddy, taco historian and professor of computer science at the Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Anantapuramu, says the earliest mention of tacos in India is in the national newspaper from 1987.
(Source: the britannica magazine.)
